Muhammad Ali Jinnah

Muhammad Ali Jinnah was known as "Quaid-e-Azam" meaning the greatest leader

Here is a timeline of Muhammad Ali Jinnah's life:

  • 25 Dec, 1876 - Born in Karachi
  • 1 May, 1895 - Barrister at the age of 18
  • 17 May, 1900 - Elected as magistrate
  • 1 May, 1906 - All India Muslim League founded
  • 4 May, 1907 - Became vice president of All India Muslim League
  • 23 March, 1940 - Lahore Resolution passed
  • 14 August, 1947 - Independence of Pakistan
  • 11 September, 1948 - Died in Pakistan
